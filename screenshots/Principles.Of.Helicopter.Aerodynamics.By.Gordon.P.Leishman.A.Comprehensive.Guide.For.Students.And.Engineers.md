## Who is Gordon P. Leishman?
  
Gordon P. Leishman is a professor of aerospace engineering at the University of Maryland and an internationally recognized expert on helicopter aerodynamics. He has over 30 years of experience in teaching, research, and consulting in the field of rotorcraft aerodynamics and design. He is also a Fellow of the Royal Aeronautical Society and the American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics.
